About K.O. Kouture


K.O. Kouture was created by Kelly O'Connor, who from a young age and her Grandmother's love and guidance grew to love sewing and fashion.

At 7 years old, her grandma taught her and her sister, how to sew by hand. She began with creating clothes for her stuffed animals and Barbies. The better Kelly got with hand sewing, the more her Grandmother, Dolores taught her. Dolores taught Kelly how to embroider by hand and by age 9, how to sew with a sewing machine. At age 9 Kelly's father bought her a sewing machine. Kelly and her sister would spend hours sewing together. Creating adorable fashions for themselves, thier stuffed animals and thier Barbies.



As she got older, she read and studied new sewing techniques. Kelly would sew clothes for herself and her freinds and family, putting her newly learned techniques to use.



In time, Kelly started her own fashion business, K.O. Kouture. She continues to learn and grow her sewing and fashion knowledge. Trying new techniques, playing with color, fabric and adornments.
